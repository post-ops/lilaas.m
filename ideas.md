# Lilaas AS – Design Ideas

## Chosen Approach: Industrial Precision Dark

**Design Movement:** Industrial Brutalism meets High-Tech Maritime Engineering

**Core Principles:**
1. Dark steel-grey palette evoking ship bridges and precision machinery
2. Orange energy accents (#FF8C00) mirroring Lilaas' brand identity
3. Asymmetric, engineering-blueprint-inspired layouts
4. 3D depth through Three.js particle fields and rotating product models

**Color Philosophy:**
- Background: Deep steel #1a2332 → #0d1520 (dark navy-charcoal)
- Primary accent: Lilaas orange #FF8C00 / #F5A623
- Secondary: Cool steel grey #4a6080
- Text: Off-white #E8EDF2 for body, pure white for headings
- Glow: Subtle orange glow on interactive elements

**Layout Paradigm:**
- Full-viewport hero with 3D animated canvas
- Diagonal section dividers (clip-path) for dynamic flow
- Left-anchored asymmetric content blocks
- Floating product cards with depth shadows

**Signature Elements:**
1. Three.js particle field simulating ocean/space environment
2. Rotating 3D wireframe control lever in hero
3. Orange scan-line effect on section entries

**Interaction Philosophy:**
- Scroll-triggered reveal animations (GSAP ScrollTrigger)
- Magnetic hover on CTA buttons
- Parallax depth on product images

**Animation:**
- Hero: floating particles + rotating 3D object
- Sections: staggered fade-up on scroll
- Numbers: count-up animation for stats
- Navigation: smooth morphing underline

**Typography System:**
- Display: Space Grotesk (bold, geometric, technical)
- Body: DM Sans (clean, readable)
- Mono accents: JetBrains Mono for technical specs
